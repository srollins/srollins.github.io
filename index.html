Bio Curriculum Vitae

I am a Professor of Computer Science at the University of San Francisco where I teach classes in the areas of software development and distributed computing. For the 2020/2021 academic year I also hold a part-time, visiting position at Northeastern University where I collaborate on research to promote greater diversity and inclusion in computing through improved training of teaching assistants and peer tutors. I recently completed a rotation as a Program Director in the Division of Undergraduate Education at the National Science Foundation where I co-led the NSF Scholarships in STEM (S-STEM) program and worked on the Improving Undergraduate Education program and the Advanced Technical Education program. I am pleased to be part of the core team supporting the USF Community Engaged Scholars in Computer Science S-STEM scholarship program.
